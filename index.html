About Us

We provide high-quality tea sourced from the finest plantations. Our commitment to quality ensures the best taste and aroma. Started in 2000, “Ramdev Tea Company” is a tea manufacturing brand located in Pune.Our manufacturing unit is well equipped with advanced machinery, trained staff and the best quality ingredients to provide you with the finest product. With a customer base all over the globe, we ensure that our tea is a perfect beverage for every palate. Our products include, Ramdev Gold, Sampavaty, Puneri Mix,Green Tea, etc. Our loyal and Hardworking staff ensures that our tea is filled with not just aroma but a lot of love. With more than a decade of experience and Under the guidance of “Mr. Gehlot” we at Ramdev Tea Company have been able to serve thousands of cup of tea all over the world.
